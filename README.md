I'M SO TIRED OF WAITING FOR NEWRECRUIT TO UPDATE SO I'LL DO IT MYSELF.

A browser-based Warhammer 40,000 army builder. No backend — everything runs client-side and saves to your browser. Points come from the Munitorum Field Manual; datasheets and weapon profiles come from the BSData (wh40k-10e) catalogues.

**Factions:** Necrons · T'au Empire · World Eaters · Space Wolves · Tyranids · Leagues of Votann

---

## Changelog

### Detachments
- Build with **multiple detachments** using a Detachment Points (DP) budget that scales with battle size (2 DP at Incursion, 3 at Strike Force).
- Rules enforced: a 3-DP detachment must be fielded alone, and two detachments can't share a keyword (e.g. the two DYNASTY or HYPERCRYPT ones).
- Each detachment shows its **DP cost** and **Force Disposition** (Take and Hold, Purge the Foe, etc.).
- **Click a detachment** in your list to read its rule description, DP/disposition, and enhancements.

### Enhancements & Upgrades
- Detachment enhancements and upgrades with point costs, counted in the army total.
- Enhancement limit scales with battle size (2 at Incursion, 4 at Strike Force).
- **Per-unit eligibility enforced** — each enhancement/upgrade only shows on the units its rule allows (Characters for enhancements, the named unit for upgrades), pulled from the faction packs.

### Datasheets & weapons
- **Click any unit** to view its full datasheet: stat line, keywords, abilities, and ranged/melee weapon profiles.
- Complete weapon data for every faction, sourced from the BSData catalogues.
- **Army-wide stat modifiers** from detachment rules are applied and highlighted in green (e.g. Cursed Legion's +2 Strength to Destroyer Cult weapons).
- Stat-changing enhancements are applied numerically (e.g. Destroyer Ankh, Murdermind, Gauntlet of Compression).

### Weapon loadouts
- Set a unit's weapons via the ⚔ button — a **per-model allocator** for squads and a **pick-one combo selector** for single models.
- **Quantity caps that scale with unit size** (e.g. 1 Venom cannon at 3 models, 2 at 6), plus per-slot totals (e.g. "1 heavy weapon per 5 models").
- Loadouts can be chosen when adding a unit, and edited afterward.
- The datasheet view filters to the weapons (and gear, like the Lychguard dispersion shield) you actually selected.

### Leaders, support & restricted units
- Attach Leader/Support characters to eligible bodyguard units; the combined unit is shown together.
- Detachment-gated units — e.g. World Eaters Blood Legion daemons (Bloodthirster, Skarbrand, Bloodletters, Bloodcrushers, Flesh Hounds) only become selectable with the Khorne Daemonkin detachment.

### Lists & sharing
- Auto-saves your current list to the browser and restores it on reload.
- **Named saved lists** — save, load, and delete multiple armies.
- **Share codes** — export an entire army (units, loadouts, detachments, enhancements, attachments) to a code and import it back.

### Interface
- **Mobile-responsive** layout (stacked columns, full-width modals, touch-friendly controls).
- Selection list **grouped by keyword** in order: Epic Hero, Character, Battle Line, Infantry, Swarm, Mounted, Beast, Monster, Vehicle, Dedicated Transport (Epic Hero and Character take precedence).
